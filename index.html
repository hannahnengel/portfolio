ABOUT ME

I am a marketing product management professional with a passion for people, culture, and collaborating with others to achieve common goals. I thrive with the fine print, actualizing projects to their fullest potential.

↑ That’s what you would read off my resume, anyway. What you WON'T understand from my resume is my absolute passion for languages. Since I was young, I studied opera, singing in 5 different languages (Italian, German, Spanish, French and English). I loved the French repertoire so much that I decided to take it to the next level and pursue it as a second major in college. I studied abroad in France, became fluent, and since then, my love for languages has only grown – I am in the process of learning German, and now, programming.

My decision to take on programming is a direct translation of my love for language and applying those skills in a meaningful way. After having been a marketing product manager in the consumer product industry for the past 3.5 years, I felt like I wasn’t living up to my full potential or using the skills that meant most to me (aside from proof-reading some French copy on packaging).

Language is all about meaning. That is why my goal is to master programming so that each day I can wake up and feel the spark of breathing meaning into my interactions, what I do, and what I create – ultimately conveying languages which directly translate to functional websites or applications.
